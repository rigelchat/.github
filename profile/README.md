## 📖 About

Rigel is a minimalist, self-hosted chat platform designed to give you complete ownership over your communications.

By combining a familiar user experience with a lightweight architecture, Rigel offers a private, fast, and independent space for communities.

No tracking, no complex overhead. Just your data, hosted on your terms.

## 🎯 Goals

- **Self-Hostable:** Your own server, your own data.
- **Easy to Deploy:** Rigel provides ready-to-use automated scripts to make installing your ecosystem effortless.
- **Discord-Compatible API:** Don't lose your favorite tools. Rigel is designed to be compatible with the Discord API, allowing you to easily port your existing bots and integrations.
- **Minimalist:** Zero bloat, no ads, and no tracking. Just pure, uninterrupted communication.
- **Open Source:** Built by the community, for the community.

## 🚀 From a Fun Experiment to a Serious Ambition

The project originally began under the name **Oxicord**.

At first, it was just a fun experiment-a way to learn and see if I could build a functional chat platform from scratch.

However, as the prototype evolved, so did the vision. I realized that this "toy project" had the potential to become a robust, serious alternative for communities seeking independence.

That's when I decided to rebrand and drop the "cord" suffix.

In 2026, I started learning **Rust** to rewrite the server from scratch (which was previously built in Node.js). This transition ensures ultimate performance, safety, and scalability for the future of the platform.

## 🦁 Why Rigel & Leo?

* **Rigel (The project):** Named after the brightest star in the [Orion constellation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Orion_(constellation)).
  It is a blue supergiant-powerful, luminous, and visible from everywhere.
  It symbolizes our goal to be a fast and brilliant alternative in the chat ecosystem.
* **Leo (The Mascot):** I named our astronaut lion (our small cat) **Leo**. While Rigel is our star, Leo (the Lion constellation) represents the feline spirit of independence and agility.
